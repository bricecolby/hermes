## B2 Grammar

- Verbs
  - Participles (present, past, active, passive)
  - Gerunds (imperfective & perfective) to form adverbial participles
- Complex sentences
  - Use of multiple subordinate clauses in one sentence (relative, temporal, causal, conditional, concessive) with correct conjunctions and commma usage.
  - Mastery of conditional clauses (incl. ommited бы in spoken)
  - Mastery of concessive clauses (несмотря на то что)
  - Purpose clauses (для того чтобы)
- Modal nuance and aspectual nuance
  - Fine-tuned use of aspect in modal or hypothetical contexts (e.g., knowing nuances like должен был сделать vs должен сделать)
  - Ability to use verbs in figurate senses (aspectual pairs conveying different meanings)
- Impersonal & passive constructions
  - Frequent use of impersonal sentences for stylistic or formal effect
  - Full control of passive voice using быть + participle or -ся forms
- Precision in cases and agreement
  - Nearly error-free usage of cases, even in advanced contexts
  - Correct agreement in complex noun phrases
  - Mastery of count-form nuances
- Stylistic registers
  - Awareness of grammatical differences across registers (bookish vs. colloquial)
  - Use of negative structures for emphasis or stylistic effect (e.g., ни малейшего понятия не имею)
