## B1 Grammar

- Sentence structure and clauses
  - Confident construction of multiple sentences with multiple clauses
  - Mastery of который for more elaborate descriptions
  - Indirect statemetns and questions (e.g., я не знаю, где он живет)
  - Use of a variety of subordinating conjunctions (хотя, если, чтобы, потому, поэтому, как)
- Verb tenses and aspect
  - Solid control of aspect in past and future contexts
  - All basic verb forms are known
  - Aspect pairs for most verbs are part of active usage
  - Learners can narrate events in different time frames with appropriate aspect choice
- Verbs of motion (prefixed)
  - Intro of prefixed motion verbs and their meanings (при-, у-, в-, вы-, по-)
  - Ability to describe movement with precision: coming, going away, entering, leaving, etc. and understanding subtle differences with type of verb
- Conditional and subjunctive
  - Use of conditional/subjunctive mood for hypotheticals and polite requests constructed with бы + past tense forms
- Comparative and superlatives
  - Confident use of comparative forms of adjectives and adverbs
  - Formation of superlatives using самый or absolute forms (лучший, наихудший)
  - Able to use comparisons with чем and genitive of comparison
- Adjective
  - Short-form adjectives for common states or qualities, incl. predicative use
- Passive voice
  - Intro to passive constructions
  - Use of reflexive verbs to convey passive meaning
  - Past passive participles быть (e.g., дом был построен в 1990 году)
- Reported speech
  - Ability to recount what someone else said or asked, maintaining appropriate tense and person (e.g., converting direct quotes to indirect speech)
  - Using structures like он попросил, чтобы я... to report commands/requests
- Advanced negation
  - use of negative particles and prefixes for nuanced expression (ни-, без-, не-)
  - Maintaining correct double negatioon across clauses
- Other
  - Impersonal expressions
  - Correct use of aspectual pairs with infinitives
  - Numerals in complex contexts
  - Collective numerals
